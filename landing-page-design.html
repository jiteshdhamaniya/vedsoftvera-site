Landing Page Design

Landing pages are single web pages that appear in response to clicking on an SEO optimized search result or an online advertisement. They are also known as "lead capture page", "static page", and "lander".

Purpose and Function

The primary objective of landing pages is lead generation. These pages are typically filled with sales copy that acts as a logical extension of the advertisement, SEO link or search result. Landing pages are often linked in digital marketing campaigns such as email campaigns, social media marketing, and search engine marketing campaigns.

In digital marketing, landing pages play a vital role in creating client engagement for the company.

What Makes a Good Landing Page?

Goal Oriented

By focusing on the ultimate goal that it is trying to achieve, a good landing page's design is streamlined towards driving the interaction towards the desired objective. A good landing page starts with asking the question, "What is the goal that we are trying to achieve?" Once that question is answered, everything else must follow the answer.

Focused on the Target Audience

Good landing pages follow the core principle of being focused on the specific audience they target. Many businesses use different landing pages for different online advertisements and social media marketing links, allowing the page to be more oriented towards the target audience.

Short Sharp Content

Landing pages are made with the intention of converting the visitor into a lead or to create engagement for the business. Good landing pages use context that is short and to the point, explaining the product or offer precisely. Many landing pages use animation and videos to explain the product, which helps increase the converting rate.

Streamlined Towards The Call To Action

A good landing page drives all pathways of possible user experience towards the call to action activity, leading to better conversion rate. The primary job is not to explain the product in detail but to provide relevant information for a targeted audience and convert them into leads.

Beautiful Design

A landing page should look good and be designed keeping aesthetics in mind as the primary job is to convert visitors into leads. Without an attractive design, a landing page stands no chance in converting.

Best Practices for Landing Page Optimisation

Select after Testing

The art of creating a good landing page depends on landing the perfect copy which is short, precise and converts well. Good practice is to come up with multiple options for a landing page and decide on a final layout after testing all options thoroughly.

Using Testimonials

A good way to optimize a landing page is to include testimonials from previous clients as a form of social proof to give more incentive for visitors to reach for the call to action button. Testimonials can also include keywords for SEO purposes.

SEO Optimised

Optimizing the content on the landing page for SEO can increase conversion rates as more people discover it from organic search, and eventually increasing traffic improves its rank.
